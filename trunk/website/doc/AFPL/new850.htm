AFPL Ghostscript 8.50

Posted 11 Dec 2004 by rillian

Artifex Software, Inc. and artofcode LLC are pleased to announce the release of AFPL Ghostscript 8.50.

This is the first release in the stable 8.5x series and it includes many bug fixes as well as new features, particularly in font rasterization and handling of new PDF 1.4 and 1.5 features. See the changelog in the source distribution for details.

Downloads

AFPL Ghostscript 8.50 is available from the usual locations:

and their mirrors.
