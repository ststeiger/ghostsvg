AFPL Ghostscript 8.50

Artifex Software, Inc. and artofcode LLC are pleased to announce the release of AFPL Ghostscript 8.51.

This is the second release in the stable 8.5x series. It contains numerous bug fixes and improvements, particularly in the area of PDF 1.6 handling. See the changelog in the source distribution for details.

Downloads

AFPL Ghostscript 8.51 is available from the usual locations:

and their mirrors.
